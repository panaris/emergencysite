Fifteen years later, Emergency Help has grown to offer patients. Insurance companies and municipalities many services within the EMS industry. Emergency Help offers ground and air transport for a vast variety of patient needs, including but not limited to Advanced Life Support, Basic Life Support, and Critical Care Transport. Emergency Help is built on quality, responsibility, honesty, and respect. These qualities have earned us an outstanding reputation and helped us become the fastest growing medical transportation provider in Greece.
